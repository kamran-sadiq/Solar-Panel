On Grid

On-grid means your solar system is tied to your local utility’s GRID. This is what most residential homes will use.

Off Grid

Being off-grid means your solar system is 100% self-sustaining and not connected in any way to your grid’s power system.

Hybrid

Hybrid solar systems is similar to a common grid-tie solar system but use special hybrid inverters & batteries to store energy.

Solar Street Lights

Solar street lighting system uses the photovoltaic technology to convert the sunlight into DC electricity through solar cells.
